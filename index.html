Football - The most popular sport in the world

Football has ancient roots, with games resembling kicking a ball played in China, Greece, and Rome over 2,000 years ago. The modern game, however, was developed in 19th-century Britain, arising from medieval folk football and the rules established at English public schools. The formation of the English Football Association (The FA) in 1863, which codified the first official rules for what would become association football (soccer), marked the game's official beginning
